About Me

Hello! I’m Amhera Mae Supnet, born on October 22, 2004. I’m proud to call Baguio City home—the cool, pine‑fringed heart of the Cordilleras.

Being the middle child of three siblings taught me balance, patience, and the value of connection. Growing up, weekends at church grounded me in faith and community. I cherish those spiritual moments that keep me centered.

Music flows through my veins—I play both piano and guitar whenever inspiration strikes. I also find peace in nature; hiking lets me breathe, reflect, and marvel at the beauty around Baguio’s rolling hills and misty trails.

Today I’m studying Criminology at the University of the Cordilleras, diving into how justice works and how people can be protected. I’m passionate about making a positive impact in society.

Thank you for visiting. I hope this little site brings you closer to my dreams, passions, and everyday moments that light up my life.
